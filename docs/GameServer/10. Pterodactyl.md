## Overview
GreatHost provides full, unrestricted access to the **Pterodactyl Panel**, the industry-standard control panel for game servers. While our custom dashboard simplifies many tasks, the Pterodactyl panel offers advanced controls and a familiar interface for experienced users.

<img src="/img/GameServer/20.png" alt="Pterodactyl Access Interface" width="100%">

## Accessing the Panel
To access the Pterodactyl panel directly:
1.  Navigate to the **Pterodactyl** tab in your server dashboard.
2.  You will see your **Email Address** and **Password** (hidden by default).
3.  Click the **Copy** buttons to easily copy your credentials.
4.  Click the **Go to Pterodactyl Panel** button or visit [https://pterodactyl.greathost.es](https://pterodactyl.greathost.es).

## Why use Pterodactyl?
- **Advanced Control**: Access features that might not yet be available in the main dashboard.
- **Familiarity**: If you've used other hosts before, you'll feel right at home.
- **Detailed Logs**: View raw server logs and crash reports with precision.
